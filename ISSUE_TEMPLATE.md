Before opening an issue:

- [Search for duplicate or closed issues](https://github.com/tarotaro080808/xrp-checker/issues)

When asking general "how to" questions:

- Please do not open an issue here

When reporting a bug, include:

- Operating system and version (Windows, Mac OS X, Android, iOS, Win10 Mobile)
- Browser and version (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, IE, MS Edge, Opera 15+, Android Browser)
- Reduced test cases and potential fixes using [CodePen](https://codepen.io/) or [JS Bin](https://jsbin.com/)

When suggesting a feature, include:

- As much detail as possible for what we should add and why it's important to XRP Checker
- Relevant links to prior art, screenshots, or live demos whenever possible
